Welcome to SAMADHAN PORTAL

The SAMADHAN PORTAL allows students to anonymously report any issues they face at the orgsnization You can file complaints related to academic issues, discipline violations, hostel concerns, library issues, and more.

Upload proof such as images, videos, or documents to validate your complaint. Our goal is to ensure a fair and safe learning environment for all students.

University Rules and Regulations

Library Rules
  • Maintain silence inside the library.
  • Return books on time to avoid fines.
  • No eating or drinking allowed.
  • Use student ID to borrow books.
Hostel Rules
  • No loud music or disturbances after 10 PM.
  • Maintain hygiene and cleanliness.
  • Visitors must be registered.
  • Follow hostel in-time strictly.
Academic Rules
  • 75% attendance mandatory.
  • Plagiarism in assignments is not allowed.
  • Respect faculty instructions and deadlines.
Discipline Rules
  • Respect peers and faculty.
  • No ragging or harassment tolerated.
  • Follow campus code of conduct.
Infrastructure Usage Rules
  • Do not damage lab equipment.
  • Use sports facilities responsibly.
  • Switch off lights/fans when not in use.
