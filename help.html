Help & Support

How to Use This Portal

1. How can I file a complaint?

Go to the 'File a Complaint' page, choose the category of your issue, describe it in detail, and upload any supporting proof.

2. How do I track my complaint status?

After submitting a complaint, you will receive a Complaint ID. Use this ID in the 'Check Status' page to track the progress.

3. Is my complaint anonymous?

Yes, all complaints are filed anonymously. We do not store personal information except for email verification.

4. How long does it take to resolve complaints?

Complaint resolution time varies depending on the issue. Generally, academic and discipline-related complaints are addressed within a week.

5. Can I submit proof along with my complaint?

Yes, you can upload images, videos, or documents to support your complaint.
